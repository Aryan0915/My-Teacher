## ईरान-इज़राइल युद्ध — इतिहास और कारण


## 1979 से पहले का ईरान

1979 ki kranti se pehle Iran par Shah Mohammad Raza Pahlavi ka 
shasan tha. Unki neeti western countries ke saath thi aur 
modernization par focus tha.

Shah ne western values ko Iran mein promote kiya. "White Revolution" 
ke tahat education aur industry mein sudhar kiye gaye.

## क्रांति के बाद — 1979

1979 ki Islamic Revolution ne sab badal diya. Iran ne Israel ko 
apna dushman maan liya aur Palestine ka support shuru kiya.

## मुख्य कारण

- **Nuclear Program** — Iran ka nuclear program Israel ke liye khatra
- **Proxy War** — Hezbollah aur Hamas ke zariye ladai
- **Regional Power** — Dono desh middle east mein dominant banna chahte hain

## हालिया घटनाएं

Recent conflict mein US bhi involve ho gaya. Nuclear sites par 
strikes hue aur tension bahut badh gayi.

## परीक्षा के लिए महत्वपूर्ण

1. 1979 ki revolution ke baad Iran-Israel relations bigde
2. Hezbollah — Lebanon mein Iran ka proxy
3. Hamas — Gaza mein Iran supported group
4. Nuclear dispute abhi bhi unresolved hai
